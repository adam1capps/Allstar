The Technology

Why this scan is different

Most inspections guess at what's under the membrane. The MRI moisture scan measures it directly — reading the entire roof surface and its insulation square by square and quantifying how much moisture is present, where, and how severe. It is the same diagnostic logic as a medical MRI: see inside before you cut.

Full-roof mapping

Every square of the roof surface and its insulation is read — not the structural deck, and never just spot-checked — so no trapped moisture is missed.

Square-by-square

Readings resolve to 10×10 ft squares and a severity level — precise, not vague.

Audit-grade record

A repeatable baseline you can re-scan against to verify any work performed.
